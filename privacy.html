Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

DistriGo collects only the minimum information necessary to provide our distribution and CRM services:

  • Account Information: Business name, email address, and role designation.
  • GPS Data: Location coordinates captured during salesman field visits (only when actively using the tracking feature).
  • Order Data: Product catalogs, order details, and invoice records created within the platform.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated business metrics for dashboard reporting.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve DistriGo's services. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Your business data remains strictly private to your organization.

3. Data Security

We employ enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and strict access controls. All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard TLS protocols.

4. Third-Party Services

DistriGo does not integrate any third-party advertising or tracking services. We use Firebase (Google Cloud) solely for secure authentication and data storage, governed by Google's enterprise security standards.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request complete deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal and business data. Contact us at any time to exercise these rights.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact the developer: Mohib Khorajiya.
